GETTING STARTED

How do I create an account?

Open BALLVYN and tap Sign Up. Enter your name, email, and password, then confirm your email address. You'll receive free tokens to start using Coach IA immediately after registration.

What is the demo account?

You can explore the app using the demo account: demo@ballvyn.app. This account lets you preview features without creating a personal account. Note that data in the demo account may be reset periodically.

Which devices are supported?

BALLVYN is available on iPhone and iPad running iOS 16 or later. An internet connection is required for Coach IA, real-time duels, and cloud sync.

FEATURES

What is Coach IA?

Coach IA is your AI-powered basketball coach, available inside the app. It uses the Claude model by Anthropic to answer training questions, build programs, and give personalised feedback. Coach IA uses a token system — you receive free tokens on sign-up and can purchase additional packs from the app.

What is Scan IA?

Scan IA uses your iPhone camera and Google ML Kit to analyse your posture and movement in real time. Point the camera at yourself while performing a move and the app will detect your pose and provide instant feedback.

How do Real-Time Duels work?

Challenge other BALLVYN users to live skill duels. Both players are connected via Supabase Realtime — complete moves as fast and accurately as possible to win. Duels require an active internet connection.

How do I buy more Coach IA tokens?

  • Open the app and go to Coach IA
  • Tap Get Tokens
  • Select a token pack and confirm the purchase with Face ID / Touch ID
  • Tokens are added instantly to your account

All purchases are processed securely through Apple's in-app purchase system (StoreKit).

ACCOUNT & BILLING

How do I reset my password?

On the login screen, tap Forgot password? and enter your email address. You'll receive a reset link within a few minutes. Check your spam folder if it doesn't arrive.

How do I delete my account?

To permanently delete your account and all associated data, please contact us at the email below. We will process your request within 30 days in accordance with GDPR / applicable privacy laws.

Are in-app purchases refundable?

Token purchases are processed by Apple. For refund requests, please use Apple's official refund process at reportaproblem.apple.com. We are unable to issue refunds directly.
